About Us 

Last Updated: June 27, 2023

Our Mission

At Meedgo, we are driven by a clear mission - to revolutionize the way people access healthcare products and services while empowering local pharmacies and connecting communities. Our innovative SAAS (Software-as-a-Service) platform combines the best of online and offline retail, creating a seamless and transparent experience for users.

Our Platform

We understand that healthcare is a fundamental need, and finding the right products at affordable prices can be a challenge. That's why we developed Meedgo, a platform that offers live price discovery, allowing users to compare and access the best deals on healthcare articles from a wide range of pharmacies, both online and offline.

Empowering Local Pharmacies

Our vision is to level the playing field for local pharmacies, enabling them to compete effectively with larger e-commerce players. We believe in supporting small businesses and fostering healthy competition, as it leads to innovation and better services for customers. Through Meedgo, we provide pharmacies with software solutions that streamline their operations, enhance efficiency, and expand their reach to a wider customer base.

Transparency and Trust

At Meedgo, we value transparency, trust, and reliability. We believe that customers should have access to accurate and real-time information when making healthcare decisions. Our platform not only showcases product prices but also provides detailed descriptions, customer reviews, and ratings, empowering users to make informed choices.

Humanizing Our Brand

We take pride in the personal touch we bring to the Meedgo brand. Our founders have a deep passion for creating positive change in the healthcare industry, and their experiences have shaped our company's values and commitment to excellence. By humanising our brand and sharing our story, we build trust and connection with our customers, ensuring they feel confident in choosing Meedgo as their go-to healthcare platform.

Our Values

Our corporate values are at the core of everything we do. We believe in integrity, accountability, and customer-centricity. We are dedicated to providing exceptional service, prioritizing user satisfaction, and continuously improving our platform to meet the evolving needs of our customers and partner pharmacies.

Join Us on the Journey

Join us on this exciting journey as we revolutionize the way people access healthcare products and services. Together, we can create a more transparent, accessible, and inclusive healthcare ecosystem that benefits everyone.

Experience Meedgo

Experience Meedgo today and discover a new way to access healthcare with convenience, transparency, and affordability.
