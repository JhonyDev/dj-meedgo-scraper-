Privacy Policy 

Last Updated: May 22, 2023

This Privacy Policy explains how Dropppin collects, uses, shares, and protects the personal information of users when you use our web platform ("Dropppin") to generate content at scale using the User Generated Content method. This Privacy Policy applies to all users of Dropppin.

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Personal Information:

When you use Dropppin, we may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide, such as your name, email address, and any other information you submit while using the platform.

1.2. Campaign Information:

As a campaign owner, we collect information related to the campaigns you create, including the type of content you desire, campaign settings, and any rewards or incentives you offer to users for their submitted content.

1.3. User Generated Content:

Dropppin collects and stores the content that users submit as part of the campaigns. This may include text, images, videos, or any other media files uploaded by users.

1.4. Usage Data:

We automatically collect certain information about your usage of Dropppin, such as your IP address, device information, browser type, and operating system. This information is collected through cookies and similar technologies.

2. Use of Information

2.1. Providing and Improving Dropppin:

We use the collected information to operate, maintain, and improve Dropppin, including enhancing its features and functionality, analyzing user trends, and troubleshooting technical issues.

2.2. Campaign Management:

We use the information you provide as a campaign owner to manage and administer the campaigns you create, including verifying submissions, determining rewards, and communicating with users.

2.3. User Communication:

We may use your email address or other provided contact information to communicate with you regarding Dropppin updates, notifications, and support.

2.4. Aggregated Data:

We may aggregate and anonymize the collected information to generate statistical or analytical insights for research or marketing purposes. This aggregated data does not identify individual users.

3. Sharing of Information

3.1. Campaign Submission:

As a user, when you submit content to a campaign, your submitted content may be shared with the campaign owner and their authorized personnel for the purpose of evaluating, selecting, and rewarding your contribution.

3.2. Service Providers:

We may engage trusted third-party service providers to assist us in operating and maintaining Dropppin. These service providers may have access to your personal information but are obligated to protect it and use it solely for the purpose of providing the requested services.

3.3. Legal Requirements:

We may disclose your personal information if required by law, regulation, or legal process, or if we believe such disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, property, or the safety of others.

3.4. Business Transfers:

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or any other transfer of ownership of Dropppin, your personal information may be transferred to the acquiring entity as part of the transaction. We will notify you via email or prominent notice on Dropppin of any such change in ownership or control.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

6. Your Choices

6.1. Account Information:

You can review and update your personal information by accessing your account settings on Dropppin.

6.2. Communications:

You can unsubscribe from marketing communications by following the instructions provided in the email or contacting us directly.

7. Children's Privacy

Dropppin is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals in this age group. If you believe we have inadvertently collected personal information from a minor, please contact us, and we will promptly delete the information.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated Privacy Policy on Dropppin or by sending you a notification. Your continued use of Dropppin after the effective date of the revised Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the changes.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at support@dropppin.com

Contact us at support@dropppin.com if you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy.
