Return, Refund and Cancellation Policy 

Last Updated: June 27, 2023

Returns

Initiation: To initiate a return, simply inform the pharmacy representative delivering the product at the time of receipt. They will assist you with the return process.

Condition: The returned item must be in its original packaging, unused, and in the same condition as when you received it. Please ensure that all tags, labels, and accessories are intact.

Timeframe: You have the option to return the item immediately upon checking it with the pharmacy representative during delivery.

Refunds

Payment Mode: If you made the payment using an online mode of payment, such as credit/debit card or net banking, you will be refunded directly to your bank account. Refunds may take up to 72 hours to reflect in your account, subject to the terms and conditions outlined below.

Terms and Conditions: Please note that refund processing time may vary depending on your bank and payment gateway. Additionally, the refund will be processed only after the returned item has been received and verified by the pharmacy.

Cancellation

To cancel your order, simply access your order history and click on the cancel icon. Alternatively, you can contact our customer support team at care@meedgo.com and provide them with your order details. Our dedicated team will promptly assist you in the cancellation process, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free experience.

Important Notes

Non-Returnable Items: Some products, such as software or undergarments, may not be eligible for return due to hygiene or licensing reasons. Please check the product description for any specific return limitations.

Return Shipping Costs: Meedgo covers the return shipping costs, ensuring a hassle-free return process for our customers.

Restocking Fees: We do not impose any restocking fees on returns. Your refund will be processed for the full amount paid, excluding any applicable shipping charges.

We encourage you to review this policy carefully before making a purchase from Meedgo. If you have any further questions or require assistance regarding returns, refunds, or cancellations, our dedicated customer support team is here to assist you.

Please note that this policy is subject to change without prior notice. The most recent version will always be available on our website.
